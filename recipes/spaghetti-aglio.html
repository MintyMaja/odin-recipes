Spagehetti Aglio e Olio

This is a nice italian recipe that is quick and easy to make

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until golden. Add red pepper flakes.
  3. Toss the spaghetti in the garlic oil. Add a splash of pasta water if needed.
  4. Sprinkle parsley on top and serve hot.
