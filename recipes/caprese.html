Caprese Salad

Delicous quick and easy Tomato and Mozzarella Salad

  • 2 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Arrange alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella on a plate.
  2. Place basil leaves between the slices of tomato and mozzarella.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve fresh.
