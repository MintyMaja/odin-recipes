Vegetarian Lentil Curry

Fiber rich delicous Lentil Curry

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can coconut milk (400ml)
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Rinse the lentils and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  3. Add curry powder, cumin, and turmeric. Stir for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and coconut milk, then add lentils.
  5. Simmer for about 25 minutes, or until lentils are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
